Support

If you need help with Mockup Kit, we're here to help. Reach out any time and we'll get back to you as quickly as possible.

Contact Support

Email: hi@damastechnologies.com

Please include your app version, macOS version, and a brief description of the issue.

What to include in your message

  • What you were trying to do in Mockup Kit
  • What happened instead (including any error text)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Your app version and macOS version

Common questions

Where are my projects stored?
All files are processed and stored locally on your Mac unless you export/share them.

How do I manage my subscription?
Use macOS System Settings -> Apple ID -> Subscriptions.

Can I request a feature?
Yes. Send ideas to hi@damastechnologies.com.

Typical response time: within 1-2 business days.
